‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is Hostlr free to use?

Yes! Koodaram is completely free for students. You can browse hostels, view photos, and contact owners without paying anything.

Does Koodaram check every hostel personally?

No. We DO NOT check every hostel individually. Hostels with Verified or Premium badge(s) has been thoroughly verified to be above standards.

How do I list my hostel?

Simply go to the List Hostel page, click on the WhatsApp button, and send us your hostel details. No registration required.

Do you verify hostels?

We do not verify every listing individually, but we allow users to report incorrect or outdated listings. We also promote trusted hostels based on user feedback. Additionally, we'll be featuring PREMIUM plan for hostel owners to promote their listing and to also get a verification badge. Such hostels will be thoroughly checked if they're above our standards

Can I update my listing later?

Yes! You can contact us on WhatsApp to update your listing. You can update or remove your listing any time.

How are ratings calculated?

Koodaram, as of now doesnt have it's own rating system. We use real user reviews from Google Businesses for our rating system

Does Koodaram charge hostel owners?

Basic listings are free. We offer optional premium plans for higher visibility ‚Äî see our Pricing page for details. Though we may have to charge compulsorily from the owners if the maintenance of the site becomes out of our expense criteria. Anyways, Koodaram will be 100% FREE for all students.
