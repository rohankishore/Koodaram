Frequently Asked Questions

Is Koodaram free to use?

Yes! Koodaram is completely free (free as in freedom) for students. You can browse hostels, view photos, and contact owners without paying anything.

Do I need an account to use Koodaram?

No! Koodaram is completely open ‚Äî no accounts, no sign-ups, no tracking. Just browse, search, and find your hostel.

How do you ensure data is accurate?

Our model is built on student verification. While we don't check every hostel personally, information is submitted and validated by a network of students who keep the listings up-to-date.

How do I contribute?

You can contribute by listing a new hostel, adding reviews and ratings to help verify information for other students.

Is Koodaram fully open source?

Yes! All hostel data is open source and available on GitHub. Anyone can view, verify, or contribute to the hostel listings. The platform code is also open ‚Äî we believe in total transparency.
