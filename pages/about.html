About Koodaram

What is Koodaram?

Koodaram is a student-first platform to help you find the best hostels and PGs near your college — with zero hassle. Our goal is to make it easier for students to settle into campus life with verified information, direct contact options, and no middlemen.

Why It Exists

We believe students deserve honest, commission-free options. We built Koodaram to solve the chaos of unreliable hostel listings and confusing WhatsApp forwards. Whether you're a fresher or returning student, you deserve better.

How It Works

Hostel owners can list their properties for free. Students can browse listings, compare amenities, and contact owners directly. No accounts. No fees. Just a simple, trustworthy tool.
